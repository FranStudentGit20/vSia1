About Cebu

Cebu City is a city located on Cebu Island’s eastern coast, south-central Philippines1. It is one of the country’s largest cities and is a bustling port1. Cebu City is the capital of the province and is independently and separately governed2. It is the country’s oldest city and became the first Spanish colony of the Philippines in 15652. Cebu City is known for its colonial architecture, mountainous surrounds, cultural and culinary scene, and is the gateway to both Bohol and Cebu Island3.
